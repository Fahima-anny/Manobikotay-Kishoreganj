ABOUT US

The organization "Manobikotay Kishoreganj" was established on October 10, 2019, with the noble mission of serving humanity.The organization has been dedicated to various charitable activities, including education, healthcare, disaster relief, and women's empowerment ,Tree Plantation , Environment Pollution , Assistance to Meritorious Students , Helping disadvanced Sick People. With a team of 28 committed members, including volunteers and professionals, we strive to make a positive impact on society. Our primary focus is to provide assistance to the underprivileged and contribute to their well-being.

President : Md. Mahbubur Rahman (Sagor)

General Secretary & Founder:Md. Murad Ahmed

RECENT ACTIVITIES

Ramadan Iftaar Distribution

whole 30 Days

We have been organizing this event every Ramadan since 2010. Our goal is to distribute iftar meals among the economically disadvantaged fasting individuals, despite the high cost of daily necessities. The most significant aspect for us is finding peace through distributing iftar. Therefore, we continue to organize this event every year. This time too, the iftar distribution program will continue uninterrupted in various locations in Kishoreganj city of Kishoreganj district during the month of Ramadan. However, anyone interested can participate in this service-oriented program.
